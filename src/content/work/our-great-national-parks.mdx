Drone operator on *Our Great National Parks* — the Higher Ground Productions / Wild Space Productions docuseries for Netflix, narrated by President Barack Obama. Five episodes across five continents, each profiling a single national park or protected area in detail.

I shot drone for the African parks coverage — primarily the Kenyan locations across Tsavo East and the Maasai Mara — and contributed aerial sequences to the wider African material the series synthesised across the continent.

## Production context

Wild Space Productions specialises in landscape and natural-history documentary at theatrical and high-end-broadcast tier. *Our Great National Parks* sits inside Higher Ground's slate (the Obamas' production company on Netflix), with the explicit editorial brief of treating the parks as protagonists rather than as backdrops. The drone work supports that — flying with the herds, not over them.

## Outcome

**Production won Best Cinematography (docuseries) at the Critics Choice Documentary Awards.** The award is the production's; the drone-work contribution sits alongside the named cinematography team's broader work on the series.
