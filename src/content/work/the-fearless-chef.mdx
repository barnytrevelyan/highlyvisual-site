Drone operator, aerial cinematographer and timelapse contributor across **ten episodes** of *The Fearless Chef* (also broadcast as *Extreme Chef*) — the National Geographic and Channel 4 adventure cooking series with presenter Kiran Jethwa.

The brief on commercial work like this is a multi-tool offer: drone-pilot one minute, timelapse-rigger the next, aerial photographer for the production stills the third. The series intercut location-establishing aerial with documentary-style ground coverage of the cooking; my contributions cover the aerial-and-timelapse component across the ten-part schedule.

## What the directors said

Both the presenter (Kiran Jethwa) and the director (Tom Whitworth) wrote testimonials on the work — the full quotes are in the carousel on the homepage. The headline from Whitworth: *"footage that was inherently cinematic. His work ethic is unparalleled too. I would highly recommend him!"*
