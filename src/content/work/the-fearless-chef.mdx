I covered all the drone work, time lapses, stills photography, and additional camera work across **ten episodes** and **ten countries** of *The Fearless Chef* (also broadcast as *Extreme Chef*) — the National Geographic and Channel 4 adventure cooking series with presenter Kiran Jethwa.

The brief on commercial work like this is a multi-tool offer: drone-pilot one minute, timelapse-rigger the next, stills photographer for the production assets the third, additional camera operator when the schedule called for it. The series intercut location-establishing aerial with documentary-style ground coverage of the cooking, across ten countries on the shoot schedule.
